who we are

It started with one number, 750,000 NYC residents live in a food desert. A food desert is an area without access to a supermarket less than a mile away and are usually in low-income neighborhoods. We brainstormed possible solutions for food deserts and came up with waste less, an e-grocery company focused solely on delivery. We’re based in Queens, NY and our delivery-focused business model, we’re able to deliver affordable and healthy produce twice a week directly to your home! We’re passionate about giving you an efficient and affordable grocery shopping. Give us a try, we promise you won’t regret it.
